Data Journalist


I am a recent Master’s graduate of digital/data journalism at Stanford University. For my undergrad, I majored in User Experience at the University of Michigan. I was an intern for Michigan Radio and their NPR's podcast Believed as well as a CUNY-J fellow for ProPublica. I used to be a reporter and editor for The Michigan Daily. I have covered a wide range of topics, from Title IX to prison closures to immigration to film representation. I like writing, videos, and the web.
